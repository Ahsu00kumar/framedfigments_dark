> > What types of events do you cover?

At Inframe, we cover a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate functions, birthdays, conferences, and private gatherings. We tailor our services to capture the essence of each unique event.

> > How do I book Inframe for an event?

You can book our services through our website or by contacting us directly. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons.

> > Can I customize the photography package for my event?

Absolutely. We offer customizable packages to fit your specific event needs, including options for additional hours, extra photographers, and special editing requests.

What should I expect on the day of the event?

Our team will arrive early to set up and discuss any last-minute details. We work seamlessly to document your event without disrupting the natural flow.

How long does it take to receive the edited photos?

Edited photos are typically delivered within 2-4 weeks, depending on the event’s size. We’ll keep you updated throughout the editing process.

Can we meet before the event to discuss details?

Definitely. We offer pre-event consultations to go over your specific needs, the event timeline, and any special requests.

Do you offer prints or albums as part of your package?

Yes, we offer high-quality prints, custom photo books, and albums as add-ons to any package, so you have beautiful keepsakes of your event.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation and rescheduling policy vary based on the booking terms. Please contact us as soon as possible for assistance.
